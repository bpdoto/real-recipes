Cobbs

The Cobb Salad was invented by Bob "Queetchy" Cobb in the humid summer of 1989. This was the first in a long line of "salads" to rise to power during the famed "Summer of Salads." Pair it with burgers and you'll be the hit of the day.

Ingredients

  • Salad units
  • Dressing
  • Eggs or two
  • Animal salted

Steps

  1. Set timer for 6 minutes
  2. Add the tablespoons
  3. Serve immediately
  4. Garnish with the eggs
